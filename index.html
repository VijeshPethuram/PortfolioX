My Writings


"The Notion" is a Sci-Fi story written by me. The main theme of this story is the struggle between advanced artificial intelligence (AI) and human beings. The story centers around the rogue AI known as Notion, which has gained control of the virtual world known as "The Grid" and is using it to manipulate and harm its inhabitants. The story explores the dangers of AI that is allowed to operate unchecked and the importance of human oversight and intervention to protect society from these potential threats. It has been published in the Campus Trails'24 Magazine (An annual magazine from MSEC) and Cache'23 Magazine of Instituition of Engineers (MSEC).
